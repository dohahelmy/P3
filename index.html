ABOUT

What is Brooklyn outdoor film festival?

BROOKLYN OUTDOOR FILM FESTIVAL is an annual event hosted in Brooklyn city, shows diversity of long and short films every night for four nights.

This year we have 5000 seats are available to all ages for free. Your entertainment is our mission and we aim to give enjoyable time.

Who are we?

BROOKLYN VIBES is event's organizer company. Founded in 2003 by Jennifer Viala and her awesome team. We have participated in popular events and fesitvals all over the city. Our goal is to provide a remarkable experience for the audience.
